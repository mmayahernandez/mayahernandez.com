About Me

Hi! My name is Maya Hernandez. I'm a 4th year Computer Science student at CU Boulder with a passion for design, coding, and creating impactful projects. I enjoy working on web development, learning new technologies, and bringing creative ideas to life.

Growing up, I was always drawn to both the creative and analytical sides of problem-solving. Computer science turned out to be the perfect intersection of those two interests. At CU Boulder, I've had the chance to explore everything from front-end web development to data-driven research, and each project has pushed me to grow in new ways.

Outside of my coursework, I'm an active member of Tri-Delta and the Society of Women Engineers, and I love being involved in campus life. Whether I'm skiing in the Rockies, cheering on the Buffs at a football game, or planning my next trip, I try to bring the same curiosity and energy that I bring to my work.

I'm currently looking ahead to opportunities in software engineering and UX-focused development, especially in roles where I can combine technical skills with thoughtful, human-centered design. I believe the best technology doesn't just work well — it connects with the people using it.

If you'd like to learn more about my work, explore my projects page or reach out on LinkedIn. I'm always happy to connect!
